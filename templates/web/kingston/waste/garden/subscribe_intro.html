You can subscribe to our garden waste collection service if you are a frequent gardener, or if you can’t home compost your own garden waste.

Costs

It costs £[% tprintf('%.2f', garden_costs.per_bin / 100 ) %] for a 12-month subscription. [% IF garden_costs.next_month %] From [% date.format(garden_costs.next_month.start_date, '%e %B') %], subscribers will pay £[% tprintf('%.2f', garden_costs.next_month.cost / 100 ) %] for a 12-month subscription. [% END %]

If you need a new bin, the cost is £[% tprintf('%.2f', garden_costs.per_new_bin_first / 100 ) %] for the first bin. Each additional bin ordered at the same time will cost £[% tprintf('%.2f', garden_costs.per_new_bin / 100 ) %], made up of a charge for the container and an administrative fee. Further information about our container charging can be found in our container charging policies.

[% IF slwp_garden_sacks %]

A roll of 10 garden waste sacks costs £[% tprintf('%.2f', garden_costs.per_sack / 100 ) %].

[% END %]

Delivery time

[% IF slwp_garden_sacks %]

We will aim to deliver your garden waste bin or roll of sacks within 20 working days.

The bags are biodegradable and break down with the garden waste. Store them in a dry place away from direct sunlight so they don’t start to break down before you use them.

[% ELSE %]

We will aim to deliver your garden waste bin within 20 working days.

[% END %]

We can collect

  • grass cuttings
  • garden or house plants
  • twigs and leaves
  • hedge trimmings
  • flowers and weeds
  • garden prunings

We can’t accept:

  • rubble
  • soil
  • food or household waste
  • flower pots or anything made of plastic

How often we collect your garden waste

We collect garden waste every 2 weeks.

[% IF slwp_garden_sacks %]

You can fill your sacks whenever you need to during your 12 month subscription and put them out on your next garden waste collection day.

[% END %]
