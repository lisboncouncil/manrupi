Request a change of size to a container:

You can request a larger container if you meet the following criteria:

  • Household of 5 or more persons registered at the address
  • If you have medical conditions that generate additional waste such as incontinence pads and/or dressings/bandages etc.

If you already have the Larger Brown General Waste Bin (240L) and still have excessive medical waste, please email a recent GP or hospital letter detailing the medical condition to neighbourhoodservices@sutton.gov.uk and we may be able to provide additional capacity.
